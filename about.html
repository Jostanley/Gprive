About Gprive

Gprive is a simple and modern anonymous platform built to help people connect, share updates, and manage their digital experience in one place anonymously.

The app focuses on speed, privacy, and a clean user experience. It is designed to work smoothly on all devices, especially mobile.

With Gprive, users can customize their settings, control privacy options, manage notifications, and interact safely with others.
