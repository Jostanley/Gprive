Help Center

How do I change my password?

Go to Settings → Account → Change Password and update your details.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account.

Why can’t I post?

Check your internet connection or ensure your account is not restricted.

How do I report abuse?

Tap any post → Report → Select reason and submit.

Is Gprive really anonymous?

Yes. We do not require real identity information to use the platform.
