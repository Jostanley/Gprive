Privacy Policy

Gprive is an anonymous social platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and minimizing the data we collect.

1. Anonymous Usage

You can use Gprive without revealing your real identity. We do not require your real name or personal identity information to use the platform.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum data needed to operate the platform, such as:

  • Username or display name
  • Email (only if required for account recovery)
  • Basic usage data (e.g. posts, interactions)

3. What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or request sensitive personal information such as:

  • National ID or government documents
  • Precise location tracking
  • Private messages content monitoring (unless reported)

4. Data Usage

Data is used only to improve user experience, maintain platform safety, and provide core features.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

6. Security

We use modern security practices to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure online.

7. User Responsibility

Even though Gprive is anonymous, users are responsible for the content they post and must follow community rules.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Gprive means you accept the changes.
